Berry Mascarpone Cake Recipe

Description

A decadent three layered cake - vanilla cake layered with berry filling, topped with a light and creamy mascarpone icing. Light and full of fruitful flavor, this cake is a perfect dessert for the summer.

Ingredients

Vanilla Cake

  • 2 & 1/2 Cups all purpose flour
  • 2 Cups sugar
  • 3 Teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 1 Cup milk
  • 1/2 Cup vegetable oil
  • 1 Tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 Cup water

Berry Filling

  • 1 & 1/2 Cups mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, or your favorite blend)
  • 3 Tablespoons water
  • 3/4 Cup sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons cornstarch

Whipped Mascarpone Frosting

  • 2 & 1/2 Cups heavy whipping cream, chilled
  • 1 & 1/2 Cups powdered sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 16 Ounces mascarpone cheese, chilled

Steps

Cake Layers

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and prepare three 8 inch cake pans with non stick baking spray and parchment paper. Parchment paper on the bottom of the pan will greatly help with preventing sticking.
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl then set aside.
  3. In a smaller bowl, mix the milk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and eggs.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix well.
  5. Slowly add in water to the batter and mix on low until combined. Scrape the sides of the bowl to ensure everything is well combined.
  6. Evenly pour the batter between the three prepared cake pans. Bake for 24-28 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few moist crumbs.
  7. Remove the cakes and allow to cool for a few minutes. Then move them to a cooling rack to finish cooling completely.

Berry Filling

  1. Add the berries and water to a food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Combine the sugar and cornstarch in a medium saucepan. Add in the berry puree.
  3. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly. Continue for 8-10 minutes.
  4. Once it comes to a boil, continue to stir for 1 minute. Then, remove from heat and refrigerate, allowing it to cool completely.

Frosting and Assembly

  1. Add the heavy whipping cream, powdered sugar and vanilla extract to a large mixing bowl. Beat on high until soft peaks begin to form.
  2. Add the mascarpone cheese to the whipped cream and beat until stiff peaks form. Keep the whipped frosting cooled in the refrigerator.
  3. Remove the domes of the cooled cakes with a serrated knife to create a flat work area on each cake.
  4. Place the first cake layer and then pipe a dam of frosting around the outside of the cake.
  5. Spread half of the berry filling on top of the cake inside of the dam.
  6. Pipe additional frosting onto the berry filling and spread into an evening layer.
  7. Add the second layer of cake on top and repeat the filling layer with additional frosting and berry filling.
  8. Add the final cake layer on top and smooth out frosting around the outsides of the cake.
  9. Finish frosting the outside of the cake and use your preferred tool to smooth the sides and/or add a pattern.
  10. Finish by decorating with additional icing and berries.
  11. Keep cake refrigerated until ready to serve.
