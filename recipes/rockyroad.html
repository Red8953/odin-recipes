Rocky Road Cookie Recipe

Description

Just like your favorite ice cream these cookies are packed full of almond, marshmallow, chocolately goodness. Even better, this recipe is incredibly quick and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 & 1/2 Cups all purpose flour
  • 1/4 Cup natural unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 Cup unsalted butter (1 stick) softened
  • 1/2 Cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 Cup brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 Cup sliced almonds
  • 1/2 Cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

    • To Top The Cookies

  • 1 Cup mini marshmallows
  • Extra sliced almonds
  • Extra chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (or later if you decide to let the cookies chill before baking). In a bowl combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, salt, and 1/2 cup chocolate chips.
  2. In a different bowl cream together the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar on low.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract and mix on low. Slowly add the dry ingredients and mix on low or by hand until fully combined.
  4. Use a cookie scoop to drop the dough balls onto a lined baking sheet. Press 3-4 mini-marshmallows into each cookie along with extra almond pieces and chocolate chips.
  5. Make sure the cookies are at least 3 inches apart. Place the tray in the oven and bake the cookies for 10-13 minutes. Let the cookies cool on the sheet for 10 minutes and then transfer to a cooling rack to finish cooling.
